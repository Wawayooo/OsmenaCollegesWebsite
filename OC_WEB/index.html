Our Academic Programs

Bachelor of Science in Criminology

BSCRIM

The Bachelor of Science in Criminology program prepares students for careers in law enforcement, investigation, and security. The curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills to develop competent professionals in crime prevention and criminal justice.

Students learn about criminal law, forensic science, crime detection techniques, and ethical principles in law enforcement.

Program Highlights

  • State-of-the-art crime laboratory
  • Internships with local police departments
  • Expert faculty with field experience
  • Comprehensive preparation for criminologist licensure examination
  • Strong focus on ethical law enforcement practices

Career Opportunities

  • Police Officer
  • Crime Scene Investigator
  • Security Management
  • Correctional Institution Officer
  • Forensic Science Technician

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

BSCS

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program equips students with the knowledge and skills needed to design, develop, and implement software solutions and computing systems. The curriculum covers programming, algorithms, database management, networking, and software engineering.

Students engage in hands-on projects that develop their problem-solving abilities and technical expertise in current technologies and emerging trends in computing.

Program Highlights

  • Modern computer laboratories with industry-standard software
  • Capstone projects with real-world applications
  • Industry partnerships for internships and job placements
  • Focus on both theoretical foundations and practical applications
  • Specialized tracks in web development, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity

Career Opportunities

  • Software Developer
  • Web Developer
  • Database Administrator
  • Systems Analyst
  • IT Project Manager

Bachelor of Secondary Education

BSED

The Bachelor of Secondary Education program prepares students to become effective and innovative teachers at the high school level. The curriculum combines content knowledge in specialized subject areas with pedagogical approaches and teaching methodologies.

Students develop competencies in curriculum development, classroom management, assessment, and instructional design, with a focus on adolescent development and learning theories.

Program Highlights

  • Specialized tracks in Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies
  • Early field experiences in local high schools
  • Technology integration in teaching and learning
  • Comprehensive preparation for Licensure Examination for Teachers (LET)
  • Student teaching experiences with mentor supervision

Career Opportunities

  • High School Teacher
  • Curriculum Developer
  • Educational Materials Writer
  • Training Specialist
  • Academic Coordinator

Bachelor of Elementary Education

BEED

The Bachelor of Elementary Education program focuses on preparing students to become effective primary school educators. The curriculum provides a strong foundation in child development, teaching methodologies, and content knowledge across multiple subjects.

Students learn to create engaging learning environments that address diverse learning needs and promote holistic development in young learners.

Program Highlights

  • Comprehensive preparation in all elementary subjects
  • Focus on child psychology and development
  • Integration of play-based and experiential learning approaches
  • Early field experiences in elementary school settings
  • Preparation for Licensure Examination for Teachers (LET)

Career Opportunities

  • Elementary School Teacher
  • Reading Specialist
  • Early Childhood Educator
  • Educational Consultant
  • Tutorial Center Coordinator

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

BSBA

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration program provides students with a comprehensive understanding of business principles, management practices, and organizational dynamics. The curriculum covers marketing, finance, human resources, operations, and strategic management.

Students develop analytical and leadership skills necessary for effective decision-making in diverse business environments.

Program Highlights

  • Specialized tracks in Marketing, Human Resource Management, and Operations Management
  • Case study approach to business problem-solving
  • Business incubation and entrepreneurship projects
  • Industry partnerships for internships and job placements
  • Integration of technology in business operations

Career Opportunities

  • Business Manager
  • Marketing Specialist
  • Human Resources Officer
  • Operations Manager
  • Entrepreneur

Bachelor of Science in Management Accountancy

BSMA

The Bachelor of Science in Management Accountancy program prepares students for careers in accounting, financial management, and business advisory services. The curriculum combines accounting principles with management concepts to develop professionals who can provide valuable financial insights for decision-making.

Students learn financial accounting, management accounting, taxation, auditing, and financial analysis with a focus on ethical practices and professional standards.

Program Highlights

  • Comprehensive preparation for Certified Public Accountant (CPA) licensure examination
  • Training in accounting software and financial information systems
  • Case-based approach to financial problem-solving
  • Strong emphasis on ethics and professional responsibility
  • Internships with accounting firms and corporate finance departments

Career Opportunities

  • Management Accountant
  • Financial Analyst
  • Auditor
  • Tax Consultant
  • Financial Controller

Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management

BSHM

The Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management program prepares students for careers in the dynamic hospitality and tourism industry. The curriculum covers hotel operations, food and beverage management, events planning, tourism, and customer service excellence.

Students develop practical skills and professional competencies through hands-on training and industry exposure in various hospitality settings.

Program Highlights

  • Training kitchens and mock hotel facilities for practical learning
  • Industry immersion through internships in hotels and restaurants
  • Events management projects and catering experiences
  • International hospitality standards and practices
  • Focus on service excellence and guest satisfaction

Career Opportunities

  • Hotel Manager
  • Restaurant Manager
  • Events Coordinator
  • Tourism Officer
  • Customer Service Manager
